Phillip John Usher, New York University


Phillip John Usher is Professor of French Literature, Thought and Culture, and of Comparative Literature, at New York University. Primarily a scholar of early modern French and Comparative literature across the genres, Professor Usher focuses his research in the fields of classical reception, cartography, and visual culture, most recently in dialogue with contemporary eco-theory.
